Irish Soda Bread

Ingredients

  • 5 potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup sweet pickle relish
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon prepared mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
  • ground black pepper to taste

Dirtections

Step 1

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add potatoes and cook until tender but still firm,
about 15 minutes. Drain, cool, peel and chop.

Step 2

While potatoes cook, place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil;
cover, remove from heat, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water,
cool, peel and chop.

Step 3

Combine the potatoes, eggs, celery, onion, relish, mayonnaise, mustard, garlic salt, celery salt,
and pepper in a large bowl. Mix together well and refrigerate until chilled.

Nutotional facts

Per Serving: 206 calories; protein 5.5g; carbohydrates 30.5g; fat 7.6g; cholesterol 72.4mg;
sodium 334.7mg.
